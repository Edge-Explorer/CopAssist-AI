# CopAssist AI Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

[SOP-101: Unauthorized Gathering]
Definition: A group of >10 people in a public park after 11 PM or in a restricted zone.
Action: 
1. Issue a warning via automated speakers.
2. Alert nearest patrol car.

[SOP-102: Extreme Crowd Density]
Definition: Crowd density exceeding 0.8 people per square meter.
Risk: Stampede or crushing.
Action: Alert dispatch and recommend crowd dispersal measures.

[SOP-103: Behavior-Based Threat Detection]
Definition: Detection of "AGGRESSIVE_STANCE" or "HANDS_RAISED" suspicious behavior in a public zone.
Threat Profile: High risk of assault, protest, or unauthorized conflict.
Action:
1. Trigger IMMEDIATE RED ALERT.
2. Deploy primary response unit.
3. Lock local perimeter.

[SOP-104: Routine Patrol Enhancement]
Definition: Small gatherings <5 people in residential areas at night.
Action: Log event for daily summary. No immediate dispatch.

[SOP-105: Emergency Response Protocols]
Definition: Sudden influx of >20 people in 5 minutes.
Action: Immediate high-priority dispatch. Monitor all adjacent cameras.
